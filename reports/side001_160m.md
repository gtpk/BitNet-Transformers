## Verdict: FAIL / INCONCLUSIVE at 160M -- tiny sidecar capacity is not a clear FACT lever

No rank lifts eval_panel by >=0.05 over the ternary-only baseline (0.185). rank 2's +0.037 is
sub-threshold and NON-MONOTONIC -- rank 4/8 fall back to 0.185 and rank 8's tags degrade. popqa_tight
stays near-zero (160M barely does PopQA). CE improves slightly with rank (4.058 -> 4.024), so the
sidecar helps language modelling a little but not factual behaviour (the "CE up, FACT flat" pattern).
Per the plan gate (rank 4/8 must lift FACT >=0.05 on 160M to earn a 1.1B SIDE-003), the bar is NOT
met -> do not auto-schedule 1.1B.

Caveat: the 160M predictor mispredicted the 1.1B FACT-003D outcome, so a 160M null does not
definitively kill the 1.1B sidecar -- it just means no cheap green light. Capacity is also
regime-dependent. If FACT-003H (the data lever) lands as case D (plateau), a 1.1B SIDE-003 may still
be warranted despite the weak 160M signal. SIDE-000 byte accounting is the durable positive: a
rank-4 all-target sidecar is only ~3% of I2_S target bytes, so "mostly-I2_S + tiny auxiliary sidecar"
remains cheap if a larger model shows a capacity signal.
